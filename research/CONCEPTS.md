## Goal

A thing that needs doing.

eg: "Implement feature x"

## Task

A thing that must be tracked. These can be discovered while performing a process or task
and must be separately tracked. Goals must be decomposed into tasks.

## Process

An ordered sequence of steps that must be performed in that order to complete a task.

## Step

Something that must be done.

## Principle

A collection of context that must be loaded by an agent before begining a process.

## Knowledge

A collection of context to load when referenced by a name.

eg: "load knowledge for testfixtures"


## Knowledge Source

A source for a particular collection of knowledge.
Might be a git checkout, or a remote web address.
Must be refreshed on a cadence, or when requested.
May have multiple versions.
